BIOGRAPHY

While Mahabali is a legendary and mythological figure from Hindu mythology and folklore, specific biographical details in the traditional sense, such as birthplace, birthdate, and family lineage, may not be available. Mahabali's story is primarily told through ancient texts and oral traditions, and it is more of a symbolic and allegorical narrative. In Hindu mythology, Mahabali is celebrated for his virtues, his rule as the king of Kerala, his generosity, and his encounter with Lord Vishnu in the Vamana avatar. His biography is often explored through these aspects of his character and the stories associated with him, rather than conventional biographical details. .

Onam is the most popular festival in the state of Kerala, India. Onam is the official state festival of Kerala. It is celebrated with joy and enthusiasm all over the state by Malayalees regardless of religion, caste or creed. .
